Parker is a sophomore journalism and information science major with a minor in law and society.

She has taken a particular interest in investigative and data journalism and is using her majors, courses and experience as a way to get involved in the field.

Currently on the multiplatform track, Parker has shown interest in many different mediums of journalism. She is involved in audio as a DJ of her radio show Remember When and print as she is now a staff writer at the news desk for The Diamondback.

She is going to continue to work towards her effort of using journalism as a way to help others by enrolling in a capstone for the Fall 2022 semester--Urban Affairs Reporting in Baltimore, which works on a semester long multiplatofrm project to serve the surrounding community.

Parker is a passionate reporter and dedicated writer that would an asset to any newsroom.
